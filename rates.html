Rates

Rates are tailored to your pet's needs.

For all services, you can expect updates with photos and video of your dog having a good time with us.

House Sitting

I am available for house sitting at locations within the San Luis Obispo area. The cost varies depending on the number of animals you have, but I can do anything from house visits to overnights at your house. Often times, dogs are more comfortable in their home environment. Rates will vary from $20 per day to $50 per day depending on the number of pets, care, and cleaning involved.

Dog Walking

Dog walking (or running) is available for up to three dogs at once. The rates run around $20 for a 30-minute walk, and $30 for an hour long walk. Only $5 extra per additional dog.

Dog Sitting

Dog sitting is provided in my home for friendly, house trained, small (under 30 lbs), clean, and quiet dogs. My home is a relaxed, calm environment and we want to keep it that way! If your dog meets the criteria and gets along with my Yorkie, rates are $30 to $40 per day which includes a minimum of a 45 minute walk. I am very active, so in good weather I usually take the dogs to the beach, hiking, or other fun adventures. We watch only one dog at a time to keep the environment enjoyable for all parties!
